INFORMATION ON COVID-19

Precautions

The biggest precaution that you can take is to stay home and stay inside. Avoid going out and coming in contact with other people as much as you can. Only leave the house for absolutely necessary tasks.

    DO:

  • Wash your hands regularlyfor 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub
  • Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when you cough or sneeze
  • Avoid close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell
