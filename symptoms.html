INFORMATION ON COVID-19

Symptopms

Usually a person develops noticeable symptoms after 1 to 14 days of being exposed to the virus. About 80% of the people recover from Coronavirus without needing any special treatment. The virus is rarely fatal. Old people and people with underlying medical conditions such as asthma, diabetes or heart diseases may be more vulnerable to becoming severly ill.

  • Cough
  • Fever
  • Fatigue or tiredness
  • Difficulty Breathing (severe cases)
